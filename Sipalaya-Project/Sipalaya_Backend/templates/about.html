Welcome to Sipalaya InfoTech

Who We Are

Sipalaya InfoTech, located in Kathmandu, Nepal, has been dedicated to helping professionals and students advance their careers in the tech industry for years. We provide high-quality, comprehensive training programs that equip our students with the skills needed to excel in the fast-evolving tech world.

Our Journey

  • • 2015: Founded with a vision to transform IT education in Nepal
  • • 2017: Expanded to offer specialized courses in web development
  • • 2019: Reached milestone of 1000+ trained students
  • • 2021: Launched corporate training programs
  • • 2023: Achieved 90% placement rate for graduates
  • • 2024: Expanded course offerings to include AI and Machine Learning
